Tribute to My Sweet Mum

Twelve years ago, I lost my one and only precious mum to the cold hands of death. Mum was a selfless person, she was caring loving and fun to be with at the same time she was a disciplinarian, my mum was a no nonsense person.

When i was much younger i used to think that my mum was too strict because she doesn't spare the rod but now that i'm older i realized that it was for the best. Her method of upbringing molded me into the person i am today and i give her credit for that.
